Coloring Books are Coming

  • Post author By JoyBountiful
  • Post date June 24, 2020
  • 1 Comment on Coloring Books are Coming

Over the past few weeks we have been working hard on several new coloring books that will be live in the next few days. Here’s a preview:

Halloween Jokes Coloring Book

This is a fun coloring book with 100 pages and 50 coloring activities. Each activity page has a funny (punny?) Halloween joke. Watch out parents, some of these are real groaners. The activity pages each have a fun, Halloween-themed coloring image too.

Four Seasons Coloring Book

This is a passion project of mine. I love the change of the seasons and have been working on a project related to it for quite some time. Each span (right and left pages) has an inspirational quote and a custom coloring area. The quote theme is the changing of the seasons.
